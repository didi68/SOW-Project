SISTERS ON A WALK

Have you ever experienced what it means to be injured in the battlefield? Do you have the desire to find out for yourself what following Jesus Christ means? To everyone who needs a safe space for expression, self discovery and to be all that God has called you to be.

What we do?

Build a community of women who stand up right behind their Chief Commanding Officer (God) Though beaten, battered and broken, they stand, battle ready.

Help each one discover and rediscover Father's/Mothers God has positioned around us for our life's journey, and how to submit to them.

To help us rebuild (our lives, purpose, dreams ) so as to fulfill God's assignment on earth.

It may not "seem" like it , but I pray as we make progress, each one of us will encounter our Companion.
